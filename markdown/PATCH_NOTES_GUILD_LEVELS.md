## Major Update: Guild Progression & Seasonal Competition!

We're excited to announce the **Guild Level System** - a comprehensive progression system that transforms guilds into competitive, collaborative organizations! Earn XP together, unlock powerful perks, complete weekly challenges, and compete in seasonal leaderboards!

## 🌟 NEW FEATURES

### Guild Level Progression
**Level up your guild through collective activity!**

- **20 Levels** of exponential progression (~2M XP to max level)
- **Earn XP Together** through:
  - Playing games (5 XP per game)
  - Wagering money (1 XP per $100)
  - Daily bonuses (50 XP)
  - Work shifts (2 XP)
  - Guild donations (1 XP per $1,000)
  - Guild heists (500 XP success / 100 XP fail)
  - Personal challenges (50-200 XP)
  - Weekly guild challenges (100-1000 XP)

- **Track Progress** with `/guild level`:
  - View current level and XP
  - See progress bar to next level
  - Preview upcoming perk unlocks
  - Check season peak level
  - View legacy points from past seasons
